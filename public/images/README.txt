Place your images in the corresponding subdirectories:

/products
  - coconut-oil.webp
  - aloe-vera.webp
  - lemongrass.webp
  - soap-set.webp

/services
  - thai-oil.webp
  - ...

/staff
  - sarah.webp
  - noi.webp

The application is configured to look for these specific filenames.
You can use .jpg or .png as well, but you'll need to update the database/mock data paths accordingly.
